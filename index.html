About Me

I am a passionate Game Developper who loves being put to the test with constant challenges. Why a Game Developper though ? It's simple, it offers a variety of challenges from debugging to simply finding the most efficient way to complete a requirement. I always had a passion for video games and with time I developped a keen interest toward the process of creating games. At the end of the day, it's all about that feeling of accomplishment I get when I press run after hours of hard work and devotion and it pays off by working as intended. I wouldn't see myself doing anything else
