Risk of Gauntlets

A game inspired by bomber man where you place bombs to defeat different kind of enemies that have different abilities

Content

Death

2 Lives, changing the character for a visual representation of a hit

Bombs

Player can place bombs that will explode overtime and kill enemies when their's a collision.

Consumable

Player can pick up consummable that will either increase the amount of carried bombs or the range of the explosion

Team Structure & Roles

Solo Project. Player Controller, Animations, Position Snapping to center of the tile, Collision, Explosions
